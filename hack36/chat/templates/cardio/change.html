Cardiology

CARDIOLOGY DOMAIN

Cardiology is a branch of medicine dealing with disorders of the heart as well as parts of the circulatory system. The field includes medical diagnosis and treatment of congenital heart defects, coronary artery disease, heart failure, valvular heart disease and electrophysiology.

DOCUMENTS REQUIRED

All the documents have to be uploaded in the google drive and a sharable link of the documents has to be provided in the space given below.

ECG

Electrocardiography or the ECG report has to be submitted in pdf/dicom3/jpeg format whichever is suitable to you.

Echo-Cardiography Report

Echocardiography uses standard two-dimensional, three-dimensional, and Doppler ultrasound to create images of the heart. The echo-cardiography report has to be uploaded in the pdf format.

Echo-Cardiography

It has to be uploaded in the form of images/videos whichever is available with the user.

Cardiac Biomarkers

Cardiac biomarkers are substances that are released into the blood when the heart is damaged or stressed. Measurements of these biomarkers are used to help diagnose acute coronary syndrome (ACS) and has to be uploaded in tropi/tropt/ck-mb/pdf format.
